# Enterprise SLA Policy

Enterprise authentication incidents that block privileged operators are P1
when more than 10 users are impacted or credential rotation is blocked.

P1 response targets:

- acknowledge customer within 15 minutes
- assign incident commander within 15 minutes
- provide external update every 30 minutes
- mitigation or rollback decision within 60 minutes

Customer-safe updates must avoid internal implementation details and should
state impact, current mitigation, next update time, and owner.
